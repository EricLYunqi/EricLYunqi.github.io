Hi! I'm Yunqi Li, a CS PhD student in my rookie season at the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://siebelschool.illinois.edu/), where I'm fortunate to work with Prof. Yongjoo Park. Before that, I studied Mathematics and Computer Science at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology](https://hkust.edu.hk/) and spent a semester on exchange at the [University of Waterloo](https://uwaterloo.ca/).

My research interests lie in multi-modal data management from a systems perspective. Before starting my PhD, I worked with Prof. Dong Deng on data integration, and with Prof. Xiaofang Zhou and Prof. Kai Huang on graph queries. Later, I interned at the [OceanBase Lab, Ant Group](https://en.oceanbase.com/), focusing on transaction processing in distributed databases.

This blog is where I write about research notes, systems programming, and everyday life.
