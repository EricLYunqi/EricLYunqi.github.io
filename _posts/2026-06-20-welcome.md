Hi, I'm Yunqi — welcome to the first post on this new blog!

I've spent the last few years writing mostly for other researchers: papers, talks, the occasional overly-formal README. This blog is meant to be looser than that. I want a place to write down the smaller things that don't fit into a paper — half-finished ideas about database systems, notes from things I'm reading, and updates on life outside the lab.

Roughly, you can expect posts under a few tags:

- **research** — notes and half-baked ideas related to data systems
- **life** — updates from grad school and beyond

If something here is useful or wrong, I'd love to hear about it — my email is in the footer.
